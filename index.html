Step-by-Step Guide

Your comprehensive guide to participating in the election process.

Before registering, ensure you meet the eligibility requirements:

  • Be a U.S. citizen.
  • Meet your state's residency requirements.
  • Be 18 years old on or before Election Day.

You can register to vote in several ways:

  • Online: Visit your state's election website.
  • By Mail: Download and mail a National Mail Voter Registration Form.
  • In Person: At your local election office, DMV, or armed forces recruitment centers.

Choose the voting method that works best for you:

Mail-in Voting

Request an absentee or mail-in ballot ahead of time.

Early Voting

Vote in person before Election Day to avoid long lines.

Election Day

Vote in person at your designated polling place.

Learn about who is on your ballot. You can find sample ballots on your local election office website or non-partisan resources to compare candidate stances.
